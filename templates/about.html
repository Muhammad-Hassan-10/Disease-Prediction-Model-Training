What is This System?

The Healthcare Disease Prediction System is an AI-powered application that uses Machine Learning to predict potential diseases based on textual symptom descriptions.

Advanced NLP and machine learning algorithms analyze symptom patterns

Risk Level Assessment

Our system categorizes predictions into three risk levels based on confidence scores

High Risk

Confidence ≥ 75%

Strong symptom-disease match. Immediate medical consultation.

Medium Risk

50% ≤ Confidence < 75%

Moderate symptom correlation. Monitor symptoms and consult if worsens.

Low Risk

Confidence < 50%

Symptoms may be mild or non-specific. General health monitoring advised.
